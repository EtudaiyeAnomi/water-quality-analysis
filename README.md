# Water Quality Analysis — Abuja, Nigeria

**Author:** Etudaiye Anomi | SHESTCO Chemistry Research Centre

Physicochemical and elemental analysis of water samples across 11 sites
in Abuja — comparing Rainy Season (July 2023) and Dry Season (March 2024).
